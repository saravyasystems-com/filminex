# Product Principles

## No use. No see.

Hide unused, unavailable, or irrelevant functionality. Progressive disclosure is a product rule, not cosmetic cleanup.

## Structured knowledge over opaque text

Use explicit properties for repeatable creative decisions. Free text remains available for story, dialogue, narration, and director intent, but should not become the only source of truth.

## Explain every value

Properties can be entered, inferred, inherited, or overridden. Preserve provenance and provide a direct route to the source.

## Identity and context are different

Character identity belongs to the reusable character. Clothing, placement, action, expression, and other scene conditions belong to the scene or lower production level.

## AI assists; talent decides

AI may propose structure and properties. The user reviews, corrects, locks, or rejects them. Provider-specific behavior must not leak into the core domain.

## Complexity belongs behind simple workflows

Deep production knowledge should be available without making the interface feel like a database.
