# Product Charter

## Vision

Filminex is the filmmaking workspace where talents transform storylines into cinematic productions.

## Who it serves

Writers, directors, producers, artists, technicians, and collaborators working in real, AI-assisted, hybrid, or animated production.

## Core problem

Creative knowledge is fragmented across scripts, prompts, images, spreadsheets, editing tools, and individual memory. Filminex makes that knowledge structured, reusable, explainable, and production-ready without forcing every user to see every capability.

## Product promise

- Develop a story from concept through episodes, scenes, shots, frames, and takes.
- Maintain consistent characters, locations, wardrobe, and production properties.
- Reverse-populate structured properties from scripts and reference images.
- Generate provider-independent prompts before introducing direct integrations.
- Support collaboration, provenance, and controlled refinement.
- Allow plans without AI or animation; advanced studios remain optional add-ons.

## Initial boundaries

The first product phase will not commit to deep integrations with editing or 3D tools. It will establish the production knowledge model, workflows, prompt output, collaboration foundations, and provider abstractions.

## Success signals

- A creator can structure a story into production units without losing context.
- A reused character retains identity while wardrobe and scene state vary correctly.
- Every inherited or AI-populated property explains its origin.
- Users see only entitled and contextually relevant capabilities.
- A project can move between real, AI, hybrid, and animation modes at scene level.
