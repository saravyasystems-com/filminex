# ADR-0021: Intellectual-property workstream

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-19

## Context

MTI and Deepfake Icon work requires deliberate design and protection analysis without prematurely selecting a legal mechanism.

## Decision

Sprint 0 includes planning for icon design, intellectual-property strategy, standards strategy, prior-art research, and trademark, patent, certification-mark, and design analysis.

The final legal protection mechanism, licensing model, ownership structure, and public-domain or defensive-publication strategy remain unresolved. Repository documents must state that they are not legal conclusions.

## Consequences

- Design and standards work proceeds with evidence rather than assumptions.
- No patent, trademark, certification, or ownership claim is frozen by this ADR.
- This ADR remains Accepted until the product owner approves a mature strategy.
