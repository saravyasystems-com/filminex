# ADR-0002: Product principles

- **Status:** Frozen
- **Date:** 2026-07-19

## Context

Filmmaking knowledge is complex. The product must expose that knowledge without burdening users with irrelevant functions.

## Decision

Filminex follows these principles:

- The complexity of software depends on the simplicity of its solution.
- Make complex knowledge explainable.
- Never make users look at functionality they are not using.
- **No use. No see.**

The experience therefore uses capability-driven UI, progressive disclosure, a minimal interface, and context-aware functionality.

## Consequences

- Capability and context determine visibility and availability.
- Automation and inheritance must be explainable.
- New functionality must not create permanent interface noise.
- A change to these governing principles requires a superseding ADR.
