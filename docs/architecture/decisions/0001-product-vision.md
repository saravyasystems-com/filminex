# ADR-0001: Product vision

- **Status:** Frozen
- **Date:** 2026-07-19

## Context

Filminex requires a stable identity and product boundary before implementation decisions can be evaluated.

## Decision

The product name is **Filminex** and its positioning is **The Filmmaking Workspace**.

Mission:

> **Empowering talents to transform storylines into cinematic productions.**

Filminex supports Real, AI, Hybrid, and Animation production. Production mode may differ at scene level. Filminex is not an AI-only filmmaking product.

## Consequences

- Product and architecture decisions must serve the complete filmmaking workspace.
- AI remains one optional production capability.
- A change to the product identity, mission, supported modes, or scene-level mode rule requires a superseding ADR.
