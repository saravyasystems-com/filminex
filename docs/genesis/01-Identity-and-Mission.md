# Chapter 1 — Identity and Mission

## Product identity

**Name:** Filminex  
**Domain:** filminex.com  
**Descriptor:** The Filmmaking Workspace

## Mission

**Empowering talents to transform storylines into cinematic productions.**

“Talents” is deliberately plural. Filminex serves the people and crafts involved in filmmaking rather than treating filmmaking as a single-user AI generation task.

## Product category

Filminex is a production knowledge workspace. It supports:

- real production;
- AI production;
- hybrid production;
- animation.

The production mode is selectable at scene level. A project is not forced into one production method.

## Position

Filminex is broader than an AI filmmaking tool and narrower than a generic project manager. It organizes the narrative, visual, technical, and collaborative knowledge required to turn a storyline into a production.

## Long-term platform context

Filminex is the first domain product built on reusable Saravya Systems platform capabilities. The platform may later support CVShot and TradingRobo. Shared engines remain domain-neutral; filmmaking rules remain inside Filminex.
